Privacy policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

The Pearson Co. respects your privacy. This policy explains how information submitted through this website is used.

Information collected

When you submit an enquiry, the website collects the information you provide, such as your name, work email, company, selected service and message.

How information is used

Your information is used to respond to your enquiry, understand your requirements and provide relevant consultancy services. It is not sold to third parties.

Form processing

Enquiries are processed through FormSubmit and delivered to Lauren@Thepearsonco.com. Avoid including confidential or sensitive information in the form.

Analytics and advertising

If you accept analytics cookies, the website may use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel and LinkedIn Insight Tag to measure website usage and marketing performance. These tools remain disabled unless configured and you grant consent.

Your choices

You can decline non-essential tracking through the cookie banner. You may request access, correction or deletion of your enquiry information by emailing Lauren@Thepearsonco.com.
